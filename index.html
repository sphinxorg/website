Coins Overview

SPX Coins

The Sphinx Protocol coins (SPX) powers staking, governance, and sovereign identity registration across the network. ICO details will be announced when the testnet milestone is reached.

SPX Overview

SPX is the native currency of the Sphinx Protocol. The base unit is nSPX (nano SPX) — the smallest denomination. 1 SPX equals 1e18 nSPX, similar to how 1 Ether equals 1e18 wei.
