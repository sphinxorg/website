◆ MISSION

Why The Foundation Exists

⚡ THE CORE MANDATE

The Sphinx Foundation exists to steward the Sphinx Protocol as a permanent public good — governed by its community, not its investors. In the era of AI and quantum disruption, the defense of humanity's digital identity cannot be proprietary, cannot be siloed, and cannot be owned by a single entity. Open code means collective security.

⚗
Open Research

All cryptographic primitives, consensus mechanisms, and identity protocols are open for public review. Security through transparency, not obscurity. Peer-reviewed post-quantum constructions.

🛡
Non-Proprietary Defense

The defense of humanity cannot be owned by a corporation. The Sphinx Foundation exists to steward the protocol as a public good — governed by its community, not its investors.

∞
Long-Term Thinking

Quantum computing threats are not hypothetical — they are engineering timelines. We build for the transition that is coming, not the threat model of today. Resistant to tomorrow's adversaries.

◎
Collective Intelligence

Diverse builders, cryptographers, privacy researchers, and identity specialists contributing from across the world. The strength of the protocol is the breadth of its contributors.
